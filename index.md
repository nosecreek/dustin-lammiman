#### Grocery Tracker

_Grocery Tracker_ is a tool for saving money on your next trip to the grocery store. It keeps a price history using API data from 15 Canadian grocery chains, and can email you updates when your favourite products go on sale! Think [camelcamelcamel](https://camelcamelcamel.com/), but for groceries. I created it to track grocery inflation and help Canadians save money!
